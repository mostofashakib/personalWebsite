By high school, that same instinct met necessity. I had dreamed of studying in the U.S. ever since 4th grade, but the cost was far beyond our means, and part-time jobs weren't even an option in Bangladesh. All I had was $5 saved from skipping lunch. Over two years, it became Bangladesh's largest eSports marketplace, and that $5 grew into more than $30,000 (600,000% ROI).
